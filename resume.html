Fellow

Code for America

2015

Our team of three built a financial wellness tool as open source software. Organizations interested in learning more about the financial wellness of those they enlist can use it freely. It is deployed as a survey and the results are presented in a aggregate form of visualized data. The product assist businesses and organizations in knowing what benefits they should offer to help people be financially grounded.
